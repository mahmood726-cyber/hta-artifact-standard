About IPD Meta-Analysis

Individual Patient Data meta-analysis is the gold standard for evidence synthesis. It enables:

  • Treatment-covariate interactions: Identify which patients benefit most
  • Subgroup analyses: More reliable than aggregate data subgroups
  • Standardized outcomes: Consistent definitions across studies
  • Missing data handling: Multiple imputation at patient level

Reference: Debray TPA et al. (2015). Get real in individual patient data (IPD) meta-analysis. Res Synth Methods.

Interpretation Guide

  • GRIM: Tests if reported means are mathematically possible given sample size
  • GRIMMER: Extends GRIM to standard deviations
  • SPRITE: Attempts to reconstruct raw data from summary statistics
  • StatCheck: Verifies consistency of reported statistics (t, F, p)

Note: Failures may indicate errors, not necessarily fraud. Always investigate in context.
